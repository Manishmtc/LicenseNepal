More about us

License Nepal is web-based software for those users or citizens of Nepal who want to know and the rules related to Nepal's transport and for those who want to make driving license.

As all we know the role and importance of transportation in our daily life. It makes our daily life easier to reach to the destination. But there are some formalities to follow given by government to follow before using transportation i.e. we must have LICENSE TO DRIVE our vehicle. So, we have made a software which help user to took LICENSE easily without the help of broker who charge heavy amount to help you.
